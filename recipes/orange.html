Orange Chicken Recipe

4-Ingridient Orange Chicken

A quick and easy orange chicken recipe with just four ingredients: chicken breast, orange marmalade, barbecue sauce, and soy sauce. The result is a tangy, sweet, and savory dish that's perfect for a simple yet flavorful meal.

Ingridients:

  • 1 lb (450g) chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup orange marmalade
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook the Chicken
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Combine
  4. Serve
