Pan-Seared Tilapia Recipe

Learn how to cook tilapia with this easy, 15-minute recipe for pan-seared tilapia that's delicious and full of flavor. This simple method of cooking fish fillets is great for a weeknight meal. Serve with fresh veggies.

Ingridients:

  • 4 (4 ounce) tilapia fillets
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Season both sides of each fillet with salt and pepper
  3. Gently press each fillet into the flour to coat
  4. Cook tilapia fillets in the hot oil, until fish flakes easily with a fork
  5. Brush melted butter onto the tilapia fillets in the last minute before removing from the skillet
